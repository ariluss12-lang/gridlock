How to Play

Fill all spaces in the grid with shapes.

Controls

  • Click / tap a cell to cycle shapes: Empty → Circle → Square → Triangle → Diamond → Star → Empty.
  • Rules are red ❌ when not satisfied and green ✅ when satisfied.
  • The timer ⏱ starts on your first move.

Solve all rules and you win!
